Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that seeking mental health support can raise many questions and uncertainties. To help address some common concerns, we've compiled a list of frequently asked questions.

What types of therapy do you offer?
We offer CBT, mindfulness-based therapy, and art therapy.
How long does therapy typically last?
The duration of therapy can vary depending on your specific needs and goals. Some individuals may find relief after just a few sessions, while others may require ongoing support over a longer period. Our therapists will work closely with you to monitor your progress and adjust the treatment plan as needed. The length of therapy is tailored to your unique circumstances and personal growth.
Do you offer online or virtual therapy sessions?
Yes, we offer the convenience of online or virtual therapy sessions. These sessions are conducted through a secure video conferencing platform, allowing you to access our services from the comfort and privacy of your own space. Online therapy can be an excellent option for those with busy schedules or limited mobility.
How do you ensure confidentiality?
We take confidentiality and privacy extremely seriously. All information shared during your sessions is kept strictly confidential and protected by professional ethical standards and legal regulations. We maintain secure records and implement robust data protection measures to safeguard your personal information.
